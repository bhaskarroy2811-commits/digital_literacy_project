Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

1. Always use strong and unique passwords for different accounts.
2. Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) wherever possible.
3. Never share your OTP, passwords, or personal details with anyone.
4. Avoid clicking on unknown or suspicious links received via SMS, email, or WhatsApp.
5. Do not download apps or files from untrusted sources.
6. Always verify payment requests before approving them on UPI apps.
7. Never enter your UPI PIN to receive money; it is only required to send money.
8. Regularly check your bank statements and transaction history.
9. Be cautious while using public Wi-Fi for financial transactions.
10. Keep your devices and apps updated to avoid security vulnerabilities.

Reporting:
If you become a victim of cybercrime, report immediately at:
National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in
